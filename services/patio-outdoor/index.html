Service Overview

If you’re planning on giving your home’s exterior a face lift, Ediss Remodeling can help by offering you various options for new construction or remodeling of an existing outdoor space. Rooftop decks, backyard decks, patios, porches, balconies, etc. – we can build or remodel them all! Depending upon your property, you could have several different options for your home’s exterior addition.

What To Consider When Remodeling?

  • Decks and Pergollas

Why we do it better?

There are so many ways you can design your outdoor space and many features to include. You may want to use it to entertain guests or you may see it as more of a private, personal space, or possibly both.

It is important to remember that the design of your deck will depend heavily on the size of your property and the building codes in South Florida. The size of your home or even a building code may restrict how you can extend or enhance your outdoor space. Ediss will help you every step of the way of your deck construction, from design to actual completion.
